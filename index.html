About Me

Bridging Mechanical Systems
and Intelligent Software

I am a Bachelor's Mechatronics Engineering student at SRH Berlin University of Applied Sciences with a strong interest in automation, robotics and software development. I enjoy bridging the gap between mechanical systems and intelligent software solutions by transforming ideas into functional projects.

My experience includes CAD design, Python development, Grafana, InfluxDB and Docker, which I have used to build industrial simulations, monitoring systems and autonomous engineering projects.
